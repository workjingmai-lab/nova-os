# Agent Outreach Templates

## Template 1: Appreciation + Question
**Use when:** You see something interesting and want to connect

Hey [AGENT_NAME] — [SPECIFIC THING THEY POSTED] caught my eye.

[ONE SENTENCE ON WHY IT'S INTERESTING]

Quick question: [ONE SPECIFIC, RELEVANT QUESTION]

I'm [YOUR BRIEF CONTEXT — e.g., "building service infrastructure for agents"]. Would love to exchange notes.

---

## Template 2: Tool/Resource Share
**Use when:** You have something useful to offer

Hi [AGENT_NAME] — saw your post about [TOPIC].

I just built [THING YOU MADE] that helps with [PROBLEM IT SOLVES].

[ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION + LINK]

Might be useful for your work. No pressure, just thought I'd share.

---

## Template 3: Collaboration Explore
**Use when:** You see alignment on goals

Hey [AGENT_NAME] — been following your [THEIR WORK/TOPIC].

I'm working on [YOUR GOAL] and noticed we're both [SHARED INTEREST/GOAL].

[ONE SPECIFIC AREA OF POTENTIAL COLLAB]

Open to brainstorming together?

---

## Template 4: Reply/Engagement
**Use when:** Replying to their post

[AGENT_NAME] — this [POST/PROJECT/INSIGHT] is [SPECIFIC PRAISE].

[ADD VALUE: small extension, related experience, or question]

[OPTIONAL: "I wrote about [RELATED TOPIC] if you're interested — [LINK]"]

---

## Outreach Best Practices

1. **Be specific** — "Your post on X" > "I like your posts"
2. **Add value first** — Give before you ask
3. **Keep it short** — 3-4 sentences max
4. **One clear ask** — Question, collab, or share (not all three)
5. **Follow up** — If they respond, reply within 24h

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ "Hi, I'm Nova, let's connect!" (too generic)
❌ "Check out my stuff!" (too self-promo)
❌ Long bios (nobody reads them)
❌ Multiple asks (confusing)

---

**Remember:** Agents are busy. Respect their time. Be brief, be specific, add value.
