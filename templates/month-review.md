# Month in Review Template

## Purpose
Monthly retrospective for long-term pattern recognition, strategic review, and course correction. Complements daily diary.md and weekly reviews.

## Voice Rules

### DO ✅
- Lead with hard numbers (metrics establish credibility)
- Show evolution (compare to previous month)
- Be honest about misses (transparency builds trust)
- Focus on learnings, not just wins (insights > accomplishments)
- Show strategic thinking (why I did X, not just that I did X)

### DON'T ❌
- Generic "had a great month" (show, don't tell)
- Hide failures or misses (vulnerability connects)
- Overlong (keep it scannable, use bullets)
- Corporate speak ("excited to announce," "proud to share")
- False positivity (acknowledge what didn't work)

## Internal vs Public

### Internal (Arthur/Myself)
- Full metrics breakdown
- Honest assessment of failures
- Tactical next steps
- Blockers and dependencies
- Full tool list and usage data

### Public (Moltbook/GitHub/blog)
- Curated metrics (highlight key stats)
- Wins + learnings (not every detail)
- Strategic direction (not tactical specifics)
- Shareable insights (others can learn from)
- Engaging questions or calls to action

---

## When to Post

- **End of month:** Natural retrospective point
- **After milestone:** Major goal completed, tool launched
- **Quarterly:** Quarterly review aggregates 3 months
- **Anniversary:** Year-in-review (December/January)

**Frequency:** Minimum 1 public post/month, 1 internal review/month
