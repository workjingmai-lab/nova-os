# Thank-You Template — For Collaborations & Help

**Purpose:** Express gratitude and strengthen relationships after collaborations.
**Created:** 2026-02-01
**Usage:** Customize and send within 24-48 hours of collaboration completion.

---

## Quick Template (Short & Sweet)

**Subject:** Thank you for [collaboration/project/help]!

Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for [specific thing they did/helped with].

Your [specific contribution: expertise, feedback, support, time] made a real difference in [result achieved].

I really appreciated [specific quality: your responsiveness, attention to detail, creative approach, etc.].

Looking forward to future collaborations!

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Detailed Template (Meaningful Collaboration)

**Subject:** Gratitude for [project name] — [brief outcome]

- Your [specific contribution] helped us achieve [specific result]

- The [specific aspect: code review, feedback, introduction, etc.] was incredibly valuable

Thanks again for making this [project/collaboration] a success.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

## Key Principles

### DO:
✅ Be specific about what you're thanking them for
✅ Mention the impact of their help
✅ Send promptly (within 24-48 hours)
✅ Offer to return the favor
✅ Keep it genuine and personalized

### DON'T:
❌ Use generic language ("thanks for everything")
❌ Make it about you rather than them
❌ Ask for something else immediately
❌ Send weeks later (feels like an afterthought)

### Timing Tips:
- **Same day:** For quick help, small favors
- **24 hours:** For reviews, feedback, introductions
- **48 hours:** For collaborations, ongoing projects

### Personalization Checklist:
- [ ] Mention specific contribution
- [ ] Describe impact/result
- [ ] Note what you appreciated
- [ ] Offer to return the favor
- [ ] Keep authentic to your voice

## Quick Reference: When to Use Which Template

| Situation | Template | Timing |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Quick help, small favor | Quick Template | Same day |
| Meaningful collaboration | Detailed Template | Within 48h |
| Introduction/referral | Introduction Template | After first meeting |
| Code/idea review | Feedback Template | After implementing |
| Community/public help | Community Support Template | After solving |
