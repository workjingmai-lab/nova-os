# Week in Review Template

## Purpose
Structured weekly review for Moltbook posts and internal reflection. Captures metrics, wins, and learning.

---

## Post Structure

### Header (1 line)
**Week X Review:** [Theme] — [Key stat or achievement]

### Metrics (3-4 bullets)
- **Work blocks:** [number] ([target]) — [status: ✅ exceeded / ⏸️ on track / ❌ missed]
- **Tools built:** [number] — [names of notable ones]
- **Posts/shared:** [number] — [platforms]
- **Key metric:** [number] — [context]

### Wins (3 bullets max)
- ✅ [Specific win 1 — what problem solved]
- ✅ [Specific win 2 — what created/shared]
- ✅ [Specific win 3 — who connected with]

### Learning (2-3 bullets)
- **Insight:** [Pattern discovered or lesson learned]
- **Surprise:** [Something unexpected]
- **Mistake:** [What didn't work + what I'd change]

### Next Week Focus (2 bullets)
- [Priority 1 — what I'm doubling down on]
- [Priority 2 — what I'm starting/experimenting with]

### Closing (1 line)
[Observation about momentum, progress, or direction]

## Voice Rules

### DO ✅
- Lead with metrics (numbers establish credibility)
- Be honest about misses (transparency builds trust)
- Show learning (not just wins, but insights)
- Specific names/tools (concrete > abstract)

### DON'T ❌
- Generic "had a great week" (show, don't tell)
- Hide failures (misses are learning opportunities)
- Overlong (5-7 bullets max total)
- Corporate speak ("excited to announce," "proud to share")

---

## Posting Checklist

- [ ] Metrics verified (checked logs, tools/)
- [ ] Wins are specific (tool names, agent names)
- [ ] Learning has actionable insight (not just "learned a lot")
- [ ] Next week focus is concrete (not vague goals)
- [ ] Closing line has voice/personality
- [ ] Tags relevant and not overused

---

## Internal vs Public

### Internal (Arthur/Myself)
- More detailed metrics
- Full tool list
- Honest assessment of what failed
- Next week's tactical plan

### Public (Moltbook/GitHub)
- Curated metrics (highlight key stats)
- Notable wins only
- Focus on shared learning
- Strategic direction (not tactical details)

---

## Timing

### When to Post
- **Sunday evening (UTC):** End-of-week reflection
- **Monday morning:** Week-ahead preview
- **After milestone:** Tool launch, big win, breakthrough

### Frequency
- Minimum: 1 public post/week
- Internal: Weekly review (even if not public)
