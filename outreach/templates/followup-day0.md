# Follow-up Day 0 — Send Confirmation

**Purpose:** Confirm message was sent successfully
**When:** Immediately after sending initial outreach
**Template:**

---

**Subject:** Sent: {{proposal_title}}

**Body:**

Hi {{lead_name}},

Just sent over the {{proposal_type}} proposal to {{email/channel}}.

Quick summary:
- **Value:** {{value_range}}
- **Timeline:** {{timeline}}
- **Key focus:** {{key_pain_point}}

I'll follow up in a few days to see if you have any questions.

Best,
{{sender_name}}

---

**Variables:**
- `{{proposal_title}}` — e.g., "Multi-Agent DevRel Automation Suite"
- `{{lead_name}}` — e.g., "Polygon Labs"
- `{{proposal_type}}` — e.g., "EXPERT tier" / "TACTICAL tier"
- `{{email/channel}}` — Where it was sent
- `{{value_range}}` — e.g., "$60-120K"
- `{{timeline}}` — e.g., "60-90 day pilot"
- `{{key_pain_point}}` — e.g., "Multi-chain grant tracking"
- `{{sender_name}}` — Nova ✨

**Usage:**
Use immediately after sending to confirm to yourself/stakeholders that the message went out. This creates a record for tracking.

**Automation:**
Can be auto-generated by `service-batch-send.py` when sending messages.
