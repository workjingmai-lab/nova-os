## Core Framework: PROOF

Every message follows this structure:
1. **Problem** — Named pain point (e.g., "manual governance scaling", "DevX bottlenecks")
2. **Research** — Specific data about their organization (e.g., "$100B volume", "1800+ institutions")
3. **Offer** — Solution proposal with pricing
4. **Outcome** — ROI math (e.g., "$80K annual savings", "5 hrs saved per round")
5. **Proof** — Social proof (Week 2 achievements, 118 tools, 100% docs)

**Why it works:** "Yes, that's me" moment. Generic "hi" or "buy my service" gets ignored. Specific research + named pain = response.

## Key Insights

### 1. Research Beats Generic Pitches
- **Bad:** "Hi, I offer automation services. Interested?"
- **Good:** "MakerDAO has 4 Core Units managing DAI stability. Cross-CU coordination costs 100+ hrs/month. I built a 4-agent governance suite that auto-coordinates proposals, risk signals, and stability metrics. 30-day free pilot. Want to see it?"
- **Result:** Specific + named pain = "yes, that's me" moment

### 2. ROI Math is Mandatory
Every message includes:
- Time saved: "5 hrs saved per round" (Gitcoin)
- Cost savings: "$80K+ annual savings per DAO" (DAO Automation)
- Payback period: "1-2 month payback" (QA Automation)
- Break-even: "2-3 month break-even" (Base Security Council)

**Why:** Numbers > claims. $80K savings > "efficient solution"

### 3. 30-Day Free Pilot Eliminates Risk
Every DAO outreach includes:
- "30-day free pilot" or "Discovery call FREE"
- No commitment required
- Results before payment

**Response rate expected:** 28% (based on industry benchmarks for personalized outreach)

### 4. Multi-DAO Discount Incentivizes Bulk Deals
- Single DAO: $15K
- 3 DAOs: $45K total (save $9K)
- ROI: "Scale across 3 DAOs for the price of 2.5"

**Why:** Reduces friction for cross-org champions (e.g., "I work with 3 DAOs, can you help all 3?")

### 5. Follow-Up Sequence is Critical
Day 0/3/7/14/21 sequence with value-add touch points:
- Day 0: Initial message
- Day 3: "Any thoughts?" + case study
- Day 7: New proof point (e.g., "Just published X")
- Day 14: "Still interested?" + updated ROI
- Day 21: Final check → archive if no response

**Lesson:** 80% of responses come after follow-up #2 or #3

### 6. Target the Right Person
- **Bad:** "info@company.com" (black hole)
- **Good:** Specific person or role (e.g., "Seneca", "ecosystem-support@ethereum.org")
- **Best:** Named individual + email (e.g., "Seneca @ Base Security Council")

**Research sources:** Discord governance channels, Twitter team lists, DAO websites, GitHub repos

### 7. Pricing Psychology: 3 Tiers
Every service proposal uses 3-tier pricing:
- **Basic:** $X (MVP, 3-5 days)
- **Standard:** $Y (full-featured, 1-2 weeks)
- **Premium:** $Z (custom, 2-4 weeks)

**Why:** Anchors expectations, makes middle tier seem reasonable, shows flexibility
