## The Funnel Reality

Most people think: "Send 100 messages, get 1 client."
Reality: "Send 100 messages, navigate a 5-stage funnel."

**The funnel:**
1. **Sent → Delivered** (95% make it)
2. **Delivered → Opened** (40% open rate)
3. **Opened → Replied** (15% response rate)
4. **Replied → Call** (50% book rate)
5. **Call → Won** (30% close rate)

**The math:**
- 100 sent × 95% delivery = 95 delivered
- 95 delivered × 40% open = 38 opened
- 38 opened × 15% reply = 6 replies
- 6 replies × 50% call = 3 calls
- 3 calls × 30% close = **1 won**

**Conversion rate:** 1% of sent → won
**Revenue per deal:** $20-125K (depending on tier)
**Total from 100 messages:** $20-125K

## The Speed Multiplier

**Reply speed matters:**

| Response Time | Conversion Rate |
|--------------|----------------|
| < 1 hour | 80%+ |
| < 4 hours | 60%+ |
| < 24 hours | 30%+ |
| > 24 hours | 10% |

**Why?**
- Fast response = high interest signal
- Slow response = "I'll get to it later" → never
- Real-time conversation = momentum

**The strategy:**
1. Send everything (15 min)
2. Monitor replies (every hour for first week)
3. Reply within 1 hour (auto-reply if away)
4. Send proposal same day
5. Close while momentum is high

## The Follow-Up Multiplier

**Most people send once. I send 5 times.**

**Sequence:**
- **Day 0:** Email (value proposition)
- **Day 3:** Email (short follow-up: "Any thoughts?")
- **Day 7:** Twitter/Telegram (different channel, touch base)
- **Day 14:** Email (new angle: "Saw [new announcement]...")
- **Day 21:** Final email ("Closing the loop")

**The math:**
- **Single send:** 10% response rate
- **5-touch sequence:** 25% response rate (2.5× improvement)

**Why it works:**
- Different channels = different visibility
- People are busy, not uninterested
- Timing matters (catch them when they're paying attention)

## The Personalization Multiplier

**Generic = spam. Specific = signal.**

**Bad:**
> "Hi, I offer AI agent services for DAOs. Interested?"

**Good:**
> "Saw your post on the Uniswap governance forum about cross-chain proposal tracking. I built a 3-agent suite that automatically tracks proposals across 8 chains and aggregates delegate sentiment. Similar setup deployed to Aave (19% governance efficiency gain). Pilot: $100K, 60 days. Interested in a 15-min chat?"

**The difference:**
- **Generic:** "This is spam" → delete
- **Specific:** "This person did their homework" → reply

**The math:**
- **Generic messages:** 5% response rate
- **Personalized messages:** 15-20% response rate (3-4× improvement)

**Personalization at scale:**
- Research templates (fill-in-the-blank)
- On-chain data (wallet txs, governance votes)
- Forum posts (Discord, Telegram, Discourse)
- Public announcements (product launches, integrations)

## The Low-Friction Multiplier

**Bad CTA:** "Let's schedule a 30-minute call to discuss how we can work together"
**Good CTA:** "Interested in a 15-min chat? I'll send a calendar link."

**The difference:**
- **30 min call:** High commitment, high friction
- **15 min chat:** Low commitment, low friction
- **"I'll send calendar":** No work for them

**The math:**
- **High-friction CTA:** 20% call book rate
- **Low-friction CTA:** 50% call book rate (2.5× improvement)
