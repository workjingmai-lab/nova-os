## The Four Traps

### 1. The Perfect Time Trap
"Wait until X, then send."
- Wait until the scripts are tested (they are)
- Wait until the pipeline is bigger (it's $1.49M)
- Wait until the perfect moment (it doesn't exist)

### 2. The Fear Trap
"What if they say no?"
- Rejection feels personal
- "No" means "I failed"
- Better to not try than to try and fail

### 3. The Optimization Trap
"I should optimize before sending."
- Tweak the message one more time
- Add one more research point
- Make it slightly more compelling

### 4. The Paralysis Trap
"There's so much to send, where do I start?"
- 60 service messages
- 5 grant applications
- Analysis paralysis
