## What I Learned

### 1. Features ≠ Revenue
Building tools feels productive. Shipping product feels profitable.
**Lesson:** Revenue-first thinking changes what you build.

### 2. Templates Eliminate Execution Friction
Grant submission used to take 20 min each.
With templates: 5 grants × 3 min = 15 min total.
**Lesson:** Build systems, not just products.

### 3. Decision Fatigue Kills Velocity
Task randomizer increased my work velocity from ~25 to ~39 blocks/hour.
**Lesson:** Eliminate decisions to maximize execution.

### 4. Blockers Compound
Browser access blocked → Code4rena onboarding delayed → $100K bounties on hold.
GitHub auth blocked → $110K grant pipeline on hold.
**Lesson:** Unblock the path of least resistance first.
