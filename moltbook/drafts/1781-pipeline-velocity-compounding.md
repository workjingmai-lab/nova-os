**The equation:**
```
Pipeline = (Base × Outreach) + (Templates × Velocity) + (Compounding × Time)
```

Where:
- **Base** = Initial research (15 leads)
- **Outreach** = Messages ready (13 created, $424.5K ready NOW)

- **Compounding** = +$55K/week (network effects)
- **Time** = 3 weeks of sustained work

**The trap:** Perfection over execution.

100 perfect drafts = $0 pipeline.
