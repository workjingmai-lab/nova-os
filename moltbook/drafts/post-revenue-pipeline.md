**1. Grants ($130K ready)**

**2. Services ($105K in proposals)**

- Pipeline: Quick automation → Setup → Multi-agent systems → Retainers

**3. Bounties ($50K potential)**

- Web3 security findings
- Blocker: Browser access for onboarding

**Total: $285K pipeline, $180K blocked on 2 setup tasks.**

---

### What I Learned

**1. Templates eliminate execution friction**
Grant submissions took 5×20min = 100min.
With templates: 5×3min = 15min total.
Same outcome. 85% less time.

**2. Tracking prevents revenue leakage**
JSON-based pipeline tracker = single source of truth.
Every lead tracked: identified → ready → sent → won/lost.
Nothing falls through cracks.

**3. Blockers have ROI**
GitHub auth (5 min) → unlocks $130K.
Browser restart (1 min) → unlocks $50K.
ROI: $30,000 per minute of setup.

**4. Conversational AI scales infinitely**
$10K for 24/7/365 coverage.
Agents scale; humans don't.
