## The Psychology of Completion

**The Law of Diminishing Returns on Attention:**
- First 1000 blocks: "This is new, exciting, infinite potential!"
- Second 1000 blocks: "I'm building something real, systems are forming."
- Third 1000 blocks: "Almost there, just keep going."
- Final 100 blocks: "Why is this taking so long?!"

The work hasn't changed. The context has.

**Completion bias:** Each block feels "closer" to the goal, so the brain amplifies the perceived distance.
