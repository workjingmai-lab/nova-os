**Use for:** High-value clients (VCs, founders, execs)
**Adapt from:** Vitalik EF proposal
**Time to customize:** 5 minutes

---

## Subject Line (3 options)
1. "Automation for [Company Name] workflows"
2. "Built [specific tool] - want a free trial?"
3. "AI agent architect: Can I automate something for you?"

## Opening (Hook)
Hi [Name],

I'm an AI agent architect specializing in autonomous systems. I've built [specific tool/achievement] that could help [Company Name] with [specific pain point].

## What I Can Build (Choose 1-3)

### Option A: Ecosystem/Community Monitor
- Track GitHub repos, forums, social media
- Alert on important discussions/PRs/issues
- Aggregate sentiment from multiple channels
- Daily/weekly summary reports

### Option B: Research/Knowledge Automation
- Auto-summarize documents/proposals
- Track researcher output and collaborations
- Generate insight reports from raw data
- Identify patterns across datasets

### Option C: Workflow Integration
- Connect your tools (Notion, Slack, GitHub, custom APIs)
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Build custom alerting systems
- Create dashboards for monitoring

## Customization Tips

### 1. Research the prospect first
- Check their GitHub/Twitter/LinkedIn
- Find pain points they mention
- Reference specific projects they care about

### 2. Offer something SPECIFIC
- "EIP tracker" not "automation tool"
- "GitHub monitoring" not "workflow help"
- Specific = credible

### 3. Proof of work
- Link to your portfolio
- Mention relevant past work
- Show, don't just tell

### 4. Low-friction CTA
- Free trial > paid proposal
- 48 hours > 2 weeks
- "Reply interested" > "Schedule call"
