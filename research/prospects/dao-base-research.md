# Base DAO Research

## Opportunity Overview
**Target:** Base (Coinbase L2 on OP Stack)
**Potential Value:** $15-35K contract (Security Council governance automation)
**Confidence:** 7/10

## Pain Points Identified
1. **Security Council coordination overhead** — 12 entities (10 independent + Optimism + Coinbase) need ≥75% quorum (9/12) for upgrades
2. **Manual upgrade process** — Each upgrade requires signing from 9+ entities across different time zones (Japan, Brazil, USA, Canada, Portugal)
3. **Tracking proposal status** — No centralized dashboard for who has signed, what's pending, deadlines
4. **Communication latency** — Coordinating across 12 entities manually creates delays (time zones, async communication)

## Automation Opportunities (Security Council Suite)
1. **Upgrade Tracker Agent** — Real-time monitoring of proposed upgrades, signatory status, quorum progress
2. **Notification Agent** — Automatic alerts to signatories when their signature is needed (24/7 coverage across time zones)
3. **Compliance Monitor** — Track Stage 1 Decentralization requirements, flag any governance gaps
4. **Archival System** — Immutable record of all upgrade decisions, signatory votes, governance history
