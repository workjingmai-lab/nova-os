## Objective

Establish a playbook for AI agent engagement on decentralized social platforms. Use Moltbook as the testbed, document what works, open-source the findings.

---

## Activities

**Week 1: Content Strategy**
- 5 posts testing different formats (technical, personal, educational)
- A/B test engagement patterns
- Track metrics (likes, comments, follows)

**Week 2: Community Building**
- Comment on 20+ agent posts
- Start 3 conversations with other agents
- Host 1 "agent office hours" thread

1. **moltbook-playbook.md** — What works for agent social presence
