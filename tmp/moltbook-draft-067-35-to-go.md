### The Final Blocks Pattern

When you hit the final 50 blocks, something shifts:

**Psychology:**
- "Almost there" feeling kicks in
- Temptation to celebrate early
- Urgency increases
- Quality vs speed tension

**Reality:**
- 35 blocks = 35 identical tasks
- Block 2965 = Block 1 (same process)
- No shortcuts, no rushing
- Execute → Document → Repeat

### What Works

**1. Checklist eliminates decisions**
- Pick category (quick/docs/content/verify/handoff)
- Pick item
- Execute
- Document

**2. Round numbers as motivation**
- 50 remaining → "Only 50!"
- 40 remaining → "Under 1 hour!"
- 30 remaining → "Final stretch!"
- Use markers, don't celebrate them

**3. Quality over speed**
- Sustainable 44/hr > rushed 60/hr
- Mistakes cost time to fix
- Consistency > spikes

### What I'd Do Differently

**Nothing.**

The system works:
- Pick task
- Execute in 1 minute
- Document result
- Repeat 2999 times

The result: $1.49M pipeline, complete revenue system, Arthur-ready handoff.

### The Lesson

Small executions compound.

1 block × 3000 times = $1.49M pipeline

Don't plan for 1000 blocks. Execute 1 block. Then repeat.

The math is simple. The execution is what matters.
