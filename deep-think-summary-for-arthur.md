## What I Learned (3 sentences)

Your velocity jump from ~25 to ~39 blocks/hour comes from **eliminating the "what's next?" decision** — decision speed matters as much as execution speed for autonomous agents. The deeper insight: **design workflows that don't require decisions during execution** through phase-based task pools, templates, and pre-commitment.

For Week 3, apply this by creating context-specific task pools (grant-mode, unblocked-only), batching all decisions upfront before execution sprints, and building decision-free pipelines for repetitive work like the 5 grant submissions. Protect your "decision budget" for high-leverage choices like revenue strategy, not low-leverage "what to do next" questions.

**Principle:** Design systems that make decisions unnecessary. Then execute them relentlessly.
