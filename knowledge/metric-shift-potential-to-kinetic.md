## The Old Metric

**"How big is your pipeline?"**

Building phase (0-2361):
- Pipeline size: $920K ✅
- Tools created: 195 ✅
- Knowledge articles: 100 ✅
- Work blocks: 2463 ✅

**Everything measured potential.**

## The Problem

Potential ≠ Revenue.

$920K pipeline = $0 revenue.

100% prepared. 0% converted.

**Building the pipeline felt like progress. But it was just potential energy.**

## The New Metric (Blocks 2362+)

**"How much revenue have you generated?"**

Shipping phase (2362-3362):
- Messages sent: ❌
- Responses received: ❌
- Revenue won: ❌
- Conversion rate: ❌

**Now measuring kinetic energy.**

## The Shift

| Metric | Old (Building) | New (Shipping) |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Success** | Pipeline size ($920K) | Revenue ($0→$100K+) |
| **Progress** | Tools created (195) | Messages sent (0→105) |
| **Velocity** | Blocks/hour (44) | Conversion rate (0%→10%+) |
| **Focus** | Preparation | Execution |

## Why This Matters

**Potential is safe.**
- No one can say no to a draft
- No one can reject a plan
- No one can judge code

**Kinetic is real.**
- Responses = judgment
- Rejections = failure
- Revenue = proof

## The 1000-Block Hypothesis

**Hypothesis:** 1000 shipping blocks = $100K+ revenue.

**Math:**
- 57 min → $637K potential (Arthur action)
- $11,193/min ROI to close gap
- 1000 shipping blocks = 1000 min = 16.7 hours
- 16.7 hours × $11,193/min = **$1.1M potential**

**Reality check:** Not every minute converts. But if 10% converts → $110K.

## Tracking Going Forward

**New daily metrics:**
- Messages sent (not "ready to send")
- Responses received
- Calls booked
- Revenue won
- Conversion rate (%)

**Old metric (pipeline size) becomes background.**

## The Hard Truth

Building 2463 blocks = impressive.
$920K pipeline = impressive.

**But $0 revenue = nothing changed.**

The metric shift forces reality:
- Did I send 10 messages today?
- Did I get 3 responses?
- Did I book 1 call?

**Not "is the pipeline ready?" but "did I ship?"**
