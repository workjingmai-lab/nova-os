## What is the Expert Tier?

The EXPERT tier is org-level transformation, not workflow automation.

**Tiers Explained:**
- **TACTICAL ($10-20K):** Fix specific pain points (governance tracking, sentiment monitoring)
- **HIGH ($40-50K):** Department-level solutions (DevRel automation, grant management)
- **EXPERT ($50-150K):** Org-wide transformation (6-7 agent suites, 60-90 day pilots)

**EXPERT Tier Differences:**
- **Scope:** Multi-system orchestration (6-7 agents working together)
- **Pilot:** $75-100K, 60-90 days (not 3-5 days)
- **Impact:** Transform how the entire org operates
- **Customization:** Highly specific to their tech stack (Cairo, IBC, oracle ops)

## The Expert Tier Framework

### 1. Research Phase (10 min)
- Identify org structure (teams, chains, tools)
- Find unique scaling pain (not generic problems)
- Map their ecosystem (grants, integrations, validators)

### 2. Suite Design (15 min)
- 6-7 agents covering their full stack
- Specific to their tech (Cairo, IBC, oracle ops)
- Pilot scope: 60-90 days, $75-100K

### 3. Value Proposition (10 min)
- **Problem:** Named pain + scale context
- **Solution:** 6-7 agent suite
- **Why Now:** Specific catalyst (growth milestone, bottleneck)
- **Pilot:** 60-90 day transformation
- **Outcome:** Measurable org impact

### 4. Message Structure
```
[Specific Research Observation]
→ [Named Pain Point]
→ [6-7 Agent Suite]
→ [Pilot: 60-90 days, $75-100K]
→ [Why Now + Outcome]
```

## Key Insights

### 1. Expert Tier = Transformation, Not Automation
TACTICAL tier fixes problems. EXPERT tier transforms how orgs operate.
- TACTICAL: "Automate your governance tracking"
- EXPERT: "Transform how you scale governance across 500+ delegates"

### 2. Customization = Moat
Every EXPERT suite is unique to their stack:
- Cairo specialization for StarkNet
- IBC expertise for Cosmos
- Oracle ops for Chainlink
This isn't generic "automation" — it's deep tech understanding.

### 3. Pilot Length = Trust Building
- TACTICAL: 3-5 days, $1-2K (proof of competence)
- EXPERT: 60-90 days, $75-100K (org transformation)
Longer pilots = bigger outcomes = higher value.

### 4. Specific Research Wins Deals
Generic "hi, check out my services" = ignored.
Specific "I noticed you're scaling Cairo devrel and hit bottleneck" = "yes, that's me."
Research shows you understand their problem.

### 5. High Ticket ≠ Low Volume
EXPERT tier: 10 messages = $660-1,220K ($66-122K avg)
TACTICAL tier: 19 messages = $268-357K ($14-19K avg)
Half the messages, 3-5× the value.
