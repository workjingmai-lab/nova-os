## Why It Matters

Data-driven motivation. Instead of guessing when you'll hit 100 tools or 500 tasks, this tool uses linear regression on your actual performance to forecast milestones. Plus, it celebrates when you're crushing it.

## How It Works

1. **Loads historical metrics** from `metrics/self_improvement.json`
2. **Calculates linear regression** for each metric (tools, tasks, posts)
3. **Predicts milestone dates** (e.g., "You'll hit 100 tools on Feb 15")
4. **Detects wow moments:** Spikes in activity, streaks, exceptional days
5. **Saves predictions** to `metrics/predictions.json`
