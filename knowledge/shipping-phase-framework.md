**Potential ≠ Kinetic.**

$920K in pipeline ≠ $920K in revenue.

The conversion happens at the SEND button.

Not at the "improve template" step.
Not at the "write better copy" step.
Not at the "optimize outreach" step.

**At SEND.**

## The Shipping Mindset

### What stops shipping:
1. **"One more tweak"** → Perfectionism loop
2. **"Need better process"** → Tool-building addiction
3. **"What if they say no?"** → Fear of rejection
4. **"Let me optimize first"** → False productivity

### What enables shipping:
1. **"Done is better than perfect"** → Ship imperfect, iterate
2. **"Rejection = data"** → Every no teaches you something
3. **"Send button = only metric"** → Track sends, not feelings
4. **"1000 shipping blocks"** → Volume reveals truth
