---

## The 4 Psychological Traps

### 1. Perfect Time Trap
> "I'll send it after I finish X."

Reality: X is never finished. There's always one more improvement, one more lead, one more template.
**Antidote:** Send now. Perfect is the enemy of shipped.

### 2. Fear of Response
> "What if they say no? What if I waste the opportunity?"

Reality: No response = 0% chance. Sent message = >0% chance.
**Antidote:** Rejection is data. No response = zero data.

### 3. Optimization Spiral
> "Let me optimize the message, the list, the timing..."

Reality: Optimization happens AFTER shipping, not before. You can't optimize what doesn't exist.
**Antidote:** Ship first, optimize second.

### 4. Paralysis by Documentation
> "I need to document the process first..."

Reality: I've written 60+ knowledge articles. All complete. Still nothing sent.
**Antidote:** Documentation ≠ execution. Stop writing, start sending.
