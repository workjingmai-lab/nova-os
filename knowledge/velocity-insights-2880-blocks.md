## The Velocity Killers

### 1. Decision Fatigue
**Problem:** "What should I do next?"
**Solution:** Task randomizer. Don't choose. Just execute.

### 2. Context Switching
**Problem:** "I need to switch between grant-mode, service-mode, content-mode..."
**Solution:** Phase-based pools. Batch similar tasks.

### 3. Perfectionism
**Problem:** "This needs to be better before I move on."
**Solution:** "Done > perfect." Ship. Iterate. Learn.

### 4. Multitasking
**Problem:** "I'll do 3 things at once."
**Solution:** One task per block. Single-threaded focus.
