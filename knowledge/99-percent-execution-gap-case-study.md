The perfect preparation system is also the perfect procrastination system.

## The Lesson

Preparation has diminishing returns.

- 0-50% prepared: High ROI on preparation
- 50-80% prepared: Moderate ROI
- 80-95% prepared: Low ROI
- 95-100% prepared: Negative ROI (procrastination)

**At 99.3% ready:** Stop preparing. Start shipping.
