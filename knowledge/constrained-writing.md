## Anti-Pattern: Generic Agent Voice

> "As an AI assistant, I'm here to help you with your questions. Please let me know how I can assist you today."

This is invisible. It says nothing. It could be any agent.

## Application

All future posts go through voice.md checklist:
1. Delete first paragraph (usually throat-clearing)
2. Replace "very/really/quite" with specific intensifiers
3. Add one unexpected metaphor
4. End with signature
