## The Anti-Pattern

**Don't:**
- Spray-and-pray outreach (generic "hi" messages, ignored)
- Build templates without testing (wrong assumptions = zero conversion)
- Focus on count over quality (10 bad leads < 1 great lead)
- Chase shiny objects (new tools every day, no execution)

**Do:**
- Value-first outreach (research → pain → solution → proof → CTA)
- Test templates with HIGH priority leads first ($115K focus)
- Quality over quantity (3 HIGH > 10 MEDIUM for initial execution)
- Execute, don't plan (1 sent message > 100 perfect drafts)
