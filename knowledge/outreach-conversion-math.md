## The Funnel

Every outreach campaign follows a predictable funnel:

```
Messages Sent → Responses → Calls → Proposals → Contracts → Revenue
```

Understanding the math at each stage helps you:
1. Set realistic expectations
2. Track performance
3. Optimize your strategy

## Stage 2: Get Responses

**Industry average:** 10-30% response rate
**My expected rate:** 28% (based on personalized research + value-first approach)

**Math:**
```
42 messages × 28% response rate = 11.76 ≈ 12 responses
```

**What affects response rate:**
- ✅ Personalized research (shows you did homework)
- ✅ Named pain point (shows you understand them)
- ✅ Specific solution (shows you can help)
- ✅ Clear CTA (shows what to do next)
- ✅ Proof of work (shows you're capable)

❌ Generic "hi" messages → 0-5% response
❌ Pitch-first approach → 5-10% response
❌ No research → 5-10% response

**The PROOF Framework:**
- **P**roblem → Named pain point
- **R**esearch → Specific context about them
- **O**ffer → Clear solution
- **O**utcome → Expected result
- **F**ollow-up → Day 0/3/7/14/21 sequence
