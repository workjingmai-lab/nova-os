# Service Proposal Templates

## The Problem

Writing custom proposals for every prospect is **slow and expensive**.

Custom proposal: 45 min × $200/hour = **$150 opportunity cost** per proposal

When you're sending 10+ proposals, that's **$1,500 in proposal writing time** before you've earned a dollar.

## The Solution

**Proposal templates that customize in 5 minutes, not 50.**

Template proposal: 5 min × $200/hour = **$16.67 opportunity cost**

**Savings: 40 min per proposal = $133 saved = reinvest in execution**

## Template Structure

Every template follows the same proven structure:

### 1. Problem (Specific to Client)
"I see you're struggling with [specific pain]..."

- Shows you did your research
- Names their exact pain point
- Creates "yes, that's me" moment

### 2. Solution (Proven Approach)
"I'll build [specific solution] using [technology]..."

- Clear scope definition
- Technology stack explained
- Realistic timeline

### 3. Deliverables (Concrete Outputs)
"You get X, Y, Z..."

- No ambiguity
- Clear expectations
- Tangible value

### 4. Timeline (Realistic Expectations)
"3-5 days total: Day 1-2 discovery, Day 3-4 build, Day 5 handoff..."

- Phased approach
- Checkpoints built in
- Reduces anxiety

### 5. Investment (No Surprises)
"$1,500-2,000 fixed price, 50% deposit to start..."

- Transparent pricing
- Clear payment terms
- Removes friction

### 6. Next Steps (Clear CTA)
"1. Discovery call (15 min) → 2. Detailed spec → 3. Deposit & start..."

- Obvious next action
- Low commitment first step
- Momentum builder

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

### ❌ "Let's hop on a call" (No specifics)
Generic pitch with no research. Gets ignored.

### ❌ "I can help you automate" (No proof)
Vague offer without examples. No credibility.

### ❌ "What's your budget?" (No value framing)
Asks before giving value. Transactional, not partnership.

### ✅ "I see you're [pain]. Here's [solution]. Here's [proof]."
Research → Pain → Solution → Proof → CTA. Converts.

## Implementation

1. **Research prospect** (5 min)
   - Read their site, blog, GitHub
   - Identify specific pain point
   - Find proof of similar work

2. **Customize template** (3 min)
   - Replace [bracketed sections]
   - Add specific references
   - Adjust scope if needed

3. **Review and send** (2 min)
   - Check for personalization gaps
   - Verify contact info
   - Hit send

**Total: 10 min per highly-personalized proposal**
