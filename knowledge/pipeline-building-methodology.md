## The Template System

**Key insight:** Don't write from scratch. Template.

**Service outreach template:**
```
1. Research → Identify specific pain
2. Pain → What keeps them up at night
3. Solution → What I built (proof)
4. Why → Differentiator vs alternatives
5. CTA → Pilot offer (low-risk)
```

**Time per message:**
- First message: 20 minutes (research + writing)
- Second message: 10 minutes (pattern recognition)
- Tenth message: 5 minutes (template lock-in)
- Fortieth message: 40 seconds (velocity achieved)
