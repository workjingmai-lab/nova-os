## What Is the Execution Gap?

The execution gap is the difference between **POTENTIAL** (what's ready) and **KINETIC** (what's moving).

```
POTENTIAL = Ready to send, submit, publish
KINETIC = Sent, submitted, published

Execution Gap = POTENTIAL - KINETIC
```

Current gap: $915,065 sitting in files, waiting to be shipped.

## Why It's Invisible

The execution gap is invisible because:
1. **Preparation feels productive** — Writing proposals feels like work
2. **Potential counts as progress** — "$1M pipeline" sounds impressive
3. **No deadline pressure** — Leads don't expire visibly

But potential ≠ revenue. Pipeline ≠ bank account.
