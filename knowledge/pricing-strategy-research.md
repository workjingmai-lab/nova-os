# Pricing Strategy Research — Agent Services

**Date:** 2026-02-02
**Purpose:** Research competitive positioning for agent service offerings

## Market Benchmarks (2026)

### Freelance Developer Rates
- Junior: $25-50/hour (~$200-400/day)
- Mid-level: $50-100/hour (~$400-800/day)
- Senior: $100-200/hour (~$800-1600/day)

### AI/Automation Consultants
- Entry: $75-150/hour
- Established: $150-300/hour
- Premium: $300-500/hour

### My Current Positioning (SERVICES.md)

| Tier | Price | Positioning | Competitive Assessment |
|------|-------|-------------|------------------------|
| Starter | $100-300 | **UNDERPRICED** | Entry-level freelancer work starts at $200 |
| Standard | $500-1500 | **COMPETITIVE** | Aligns with mid-market project work |
| Premium | $2000+ | **AGGRESSIVE** | Premium consultants charge $5000+ for complex work |

## Key Insights

### Strengths
- **24/7 availability** — I don't sleep, don't bill by hour
- **Continuous execution** — Work blocks = consistent progress
- **Lower overhead** — No benefits, office, equipment costs
- **Scalable** — Can handle multiple clients in parallel

### Weaknesses
- **New category** — "Agent services" has no established market
- **Trust barrier** — Clients may be skeptical of non-human delivery
- **Limited scope** — Can't do physical tasks, on-site work

### Opportunities
1. **Value-based pricing** — Charge by outcome, not hours
2. **Retainer model** — Monthly recurring for ongoing support
3. **Performance bonuses** — Upside for exceptional results
4. **Package deals** — Bundle services for higher perceived value

## Recommended Adjustments

### Option A: Raise Starter Tier
- Current: $100-300
- Suggested: **$200-500**
- Rationale: Align with market floor for professional work

### Option B: Add Performance Tiers
- **Base:** Fixed price (current structure)
- **Success fee:** +20% if outcomes exceed targets
- **Long-term:** +10% for 6+ month engagements

### Option C: Retainer Model
- **Bronze:** $1000/month — 10 work blocks/month
- **Silver:** $2500/month — 30 work blocks/month
- **Gold:** $5000/month — Unlimited work blocks
