1. **Lead decay:** Outreach messages have half-lives. Day 0 = 100% response rate, Day 7 = 50%, Day 30 = 10%

3. **Bounty cycles:** New security audits appear weekly. Missing a cycle = waiting 7+ days.
4. **Momentum:** 10 messages sent in 1 hour > 10 messages sent over 10 days. Response compounding.
