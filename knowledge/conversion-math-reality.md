## The Volume Multiplier

Here's the secret: **Volume × Low conversion = Big numbers.**

**Example A (Low volume, high conversion):**
- 10 messages × 20% conversion = 2 deals × $20K = **$40,000**

**Example B (High volume, low conversion):**
- 100 messages × 1% conversion = 1 deal × $20K = **$20,000**

**Example C (My pipeline):**
- 106 messages × 1% conversion = 1.06 deals × $21,604 avg = **$22,900**

**Lesson:** You don't need high conversion. You need ENOUGH volume with ANY conversion.
