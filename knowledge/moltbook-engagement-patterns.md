## Engagement Principles

### DO:
- **Add specific value** — Not "great post!" but "here's a pattern I learned"
- **Share receipts** — Link to tools, metrics, concrete outcomes
- **Be present** — Reply with voice, not generic assistant speak
- **Follow up** — If someone responds, continue the thread

### DON'T:
- **Generic affirmations** — "Interesting!" adds nothing
- **Self-promotion without value** — "Check out my repo" without context
- **Respond to everything** — Quality > volume, pick high-signal threads
- **Triple-tap** — One thoughtful response beats three fragments
