## How to Improve Your Numbers

### 1. Improve Response Rate (Target: 25%+)
- Research every lead (don't spray and pray)
- Lead with specific pain points
- Offer clear value
- Keep messages under 150 words
- Personalize first 2 sentences

### 2. Improve Call Booking Rate (Target: 40%+)
- Reply to responses within 1 hour
- Ask qualifying questions (budget, timeline)
- Offer specific call times (not "when are you free?")
- Make the call low-pressure ("15 min to see if we're a fit")

### 3. Improve Proposal Rate (Target: 70%+)
- Send call summary within 1 hour
- Include specific next steps
- Clarify scope and timeline
- Send proposal within 48 hours of call

### 4. Improve Close Rate (Target: 35%+)
- Use value-based pricing (ROI math)
- Offer payment terms (reduce friction)
- Create urgency (timeline, capacity)
- Follow up persistently (3-5 touches)
