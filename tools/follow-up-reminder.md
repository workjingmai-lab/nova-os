# follow-up-reminder.py

Scans revenue pipeline for items needing follow-up based on days since submission. Generates action reminders with template suggestions.

## What It Does

Tracks follow-up schedule for submitted outreach:
- **Day 3:** Send value-add content (article, tool, insight)
- **Day 7:** Casual check-in ("Any thoughts?")
- **Day 14:** Graceful close-out ("Not a priority, I get it")
- **Grants:** Week 2/4/8 follow-ups (status, timeline, feedback)

Groups by priority (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) and provides template suggestions for each touch point.

## Why It Matters

Follow-ups recover 50% of "dead" leads vs 10% without follow-ups. Most revenue is lost not from bad outreach, but from NO follow-up. This tool prevents revenue leakage by tracking exactly when to reach out again.

## Usage

```bash
# Check for follow-ups
python3 tools/follow-up-reminder.py
```

## Follow-Up Schedule

### Services (Day 3/7/14)
- **Day 3:** Value-add content (share relevant article/tool/insight)
- **Day 7:** Casual check-in (gentle nudge, not pushy)
- **Day 14:** Graceful close-out (accept no interest, leave door open)

### Grants (Week 2/4/8)
- **Week 2:** Status check (any updates?)
- **Week 4:** Timeline follow-up (when will decisions be made?)
- **Week 8:** Feedback request (if rejected, what could improve?)

## Templates Provided

Tool includes 3 copy-paste templates:
1. **value-add** â€” Share relevant content with context
2. **check-in** â€” Gentle nudge, acknowledge busy timing
3. **close-out** â€” Accept rejection gracefully, leave door open

## Integration

Part of revenue workflow:
1. **Send messages:** outreach-batch-sender.py
2. **Update pipeline:** revenue-tracker.py update --status submitted
3. **Track follow-ups:** follow-up-reminder.py (daily check)
4. **Execute follow-ups:** Use provided templates
